> Dear all,
> 
> 
> following up on our Save-the-Date announcement: registration for the VII edition of the UniVersum meeting series is now open.
> 
> UniVersum VII — Naples (6–8 May 2026)  
> Venue: Centro Congressi, Università degli Studi di Napoli  
> “Federico II”  
> Via Partenope, 36 – 80121 Napoli
> 
> Venue page: https://www.centrocongressi.unina.it/via-partenope/  
> Conference website: http://universum.to.infn.it/
> 
> 
> *Registration*
>
> Please register here:  
> https://docs.google.com/forms/d/e/1FAIpQLSdI3SEt3-sWfKd7ceE2O1llEd_hEHFm2QTlGSrRkcPXTdlv0A/viewform?usp=sharing&ouid=109649807390177458
> 
> The registration deadline is 3rd April 2026.
> 
> *Contributed talks (abstract submission)*
> 
> Participants are warmly invited to submit an abstract if they wish to give a contributed talk. Given the limited number of available
> slots, depending on the number of submissions we may need to select among the requests. In that case, we will give priority to
> early-career participants (PhD students and postdocs).
> 
> 
> *Social dinner*
> 
> We are also planning a social dinner the first day. You will be able to indicate your participation during the registration process.
> 
> 
> *Accommodation*
> 
> Participants are kindly invited to arrange their own accommodation directly with their preferred providers (Naples offers many options
> within walking distance of the venue).
> 
> 
> Please circulate this information among potentially interested colleagues.
>
