> Dear colleague,
> 
> We are happy to announce the opening of registrations for the third edition of conference “Exact techniques and their applications -
> InTropea2026”.
> 
> The main focus of the conference will be on exact methods in theoretical high-energy physics, statistical mechanics and condensed
> matter, and their applications.
> 
> The conference will take place in Tropea, Calabria, Italy from the 28th of August to the 1st of September 2026. The registration
> deadline is the 15th of July.
> 
> You can find more information and the link to the Registration Form on the website InTropea2026
> 
> https://sites.google.com/view/intropea2026/home
> 
> Participants are kindly requested to arrange and cover their own accommodation.
> 
> For any questions, you can contact us at intropea2026@gmail.com
> 
> Please forward this message to anyone potentially interested.
> 
> Hope to see you there!
> 
> Best regards,
> 
> The organizers
> 
> Andrea Cavaglià, Rouven Frassek, Domenico Giuliano, Andrea Nava, Chiara Paletta, Marco Rossi, Roberto Tateo, Andrea Trombettoni
