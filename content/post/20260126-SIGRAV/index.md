## Scuola SIGRAV 2026 | Neutron Stars: Modelling and Detection
Vietri Sul Mare (SA), Italy, 16-20 February 2026.  
(website, https://agenda.infn.it/event/49236/ ). 

Deadline Febraury 6, 2026

The program of the School comprises the following four courses:

- Modelling of Neutron Stars as Gravitational Wave Sources  
o Lecturer: B. Haskell (Milano Univ., IT)

- Gravitational Signal from Late Inspiral and Merger of Neutron Stars  
o Lecturer: T. Hinderer (Utrecht Univ., NL)

- Detection of Continuous Gravitational Waves  
o Lecturer: M.A. Papa (AEI, Hannover, DE)

- Multimessenger Astrophysics with Neutron Stars  
o Lecturer: G. Ghirlanda (INAF, Brera, IT).


The School is intended for PhD students and young post-docs.
