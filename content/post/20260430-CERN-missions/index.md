I would like to remind you that, despite appearances, CERN is not in Geneva.  
When you open the mission, please indicate *punto di interesse* and then CERN directly.

![](images/interesse-CERN.png)

Otherwise, the mission goes all the way up to the president of INFN for authorization. And nobody is happy about that.
