> It is our pleasure to announce that the XVI International Conference on Beauty, Charm, Hyperons in Hadronic Interactions (BEACH2026) will be held in Florence (Italy) from June 8 to June 12, 2026.
> 
> This edition, hosted in the center of historic and culturally rich city of Florence, continues the BEACH series, which began in Strasbourg in 1995. The series offers a biennial opportunity for theorists and experimentalists from the high-energy physics community to discuss all aspects of flavour physics.
> 
> The abstract submission is open and will close on February 28, 2026. Contributions can be accepted as oral communications or posters. Abstracts can be submitted through the conference website
> https://indico.cern.ch/event/1537617
> 
> where all practical information regarding registration and accommodation will be provided.
> 
> For any questions, please contact the Local Organizing Committee at [beach2026@lists.fi.infn.it](mailto:beach2026@lists.fi.infn.it)
> 
> Looking forward to welcoming you in Florence!
> 
> On behalf of the Local Organizing Committee,  
> Giovanni Passaleva  (LOC chair, INFN Firenze)
