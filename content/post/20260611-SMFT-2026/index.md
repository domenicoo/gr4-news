> Dear Colleagues,
> 
> We are pleased to announce the XX edition of the “SM&FT” workshop, which will take place in Bari from 16 to 18 December 2026.
> 
> The workshop website, https://agenda.infn.it/event/49926, is now online and will be progressively updated as new information becomes
> available.
> 
> Registration and abstract submission are now open and will remain open until 8 December 2026.
> 
> The main topics will be Quantum Field Theory, Statistical Mechanics, Complex Systems, Cosmology, High-Performance Computing, and Quantum
> Computing.
> 
> As in previous editions, the main aim of SM&FT is to promote the cross-fertilization of ideas from these fields and to explore common
> computational tools.
> 
> The workshop will be held at the “Centro Polifunzionale Studenti” of the University of Bari, located in the former Palazzo Poste
> building in the city center.
> 
> We would be grateful if you could share this announcement with interested colleagues at your institution.
> 
> We hope that many of you will join us in Bari for SM&FT 2026!
> 
> The SM&FT 2026 Organizing Committee
