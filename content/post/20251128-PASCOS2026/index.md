> Dear Colleagues,
> 
> We are pleased to announce that PASCOS 2026, the 31st
> International Symposium on Particles, Strings, and Cosmology,
> will be held at the University of Sheffield (UK) from 22–26 June
> 2026.
> 
> The conference will highlight recent advances in particle
> physics, astroparticle physics, string theory, and cosmology,
> with a strong emphasis on the interplay between these fields.
> The programme will feature invited plenary talks and parallel
> sessions.
> 
> The conference website is now live, and registration and
> abstract submissions for contributed talks and posters are open:
> 
> https://indico.global/event/14705/
>
