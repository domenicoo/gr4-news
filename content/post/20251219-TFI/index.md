> Dear Friends,
> 
> we are happy to announce that the 9th meeting TFI: Theories of the Fundamental Interactions, organized by the networks GAST, GSS and ST&FI, will take place at the center Paolo VI in Brescia on 25-27 May 2026. 
> 
> The program will include three review lectures on topics of current interest, as well as talks by young researchers and a poster session. Dionysios Anninos (King's College, London) and Zohar Komargodski (Simons Center For Geometry and Physics, Stony Brook) have already agreed to participate as review lecturers. Speakers for the talks will be proposed by the Network Nodes and reviewed by the organizers. 
> 
> Registration is open at the conference website https://agenda.infn.it/event/48355/, where you can also find additional details.  
> For people staying directly at Paolo VI, it is suggested to book a room in advance (there is a free cancellation option).
> 
> As in the previous editions, the conference will be a great opportunity to bring together senior and young researchers of the INFN networks, therefore we kindly ask you to circulate the email between all the members.
> 
> Those who wish to present their work at the Poster Session are invited to send a message with a title and an abstract of their work to tfi2026@lists.infn.it with the Object: Poster Presentation TFI 2026 by April 20th 2026.
> 
> We hope to see all of you in Brescia!
>
