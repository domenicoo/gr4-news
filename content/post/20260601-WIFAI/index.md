> Dear Colleagues, 
> 
> We are pleased to announce that the 5th edition of WIFAI, the "Workshop
> Italiano sulla Fisica ad Alta Intensità" (Italian Workshop on Physics at
> High Intensity) will take place in Perugia, Italy, from 10 to 13 November
> 2026.
> 
> All details about the workshop are available at the following Indico page:
> https://agenda.infn.it/event/49033/
> 
> The deadline for the standard-fee registration is September 15th. 
> Please save the date and feel free to share this announcement with
> colleagues and collaborators who might be interested in attending.
> 
> The workshop will feature a "Young Scientist Forum", offering early-career
> researchers in the high-intensity physics community an opportunity to
> present their work and share innovative ideas.
> The deadline to submit abstracts for the Young Scientist Forum is August
> 31st. 
> 
> We look forward to welcoming you to Perugia!
> With best regards,
> 
> Francesco Brizioli  
> on behalf of the Local Organizing Committee
>
