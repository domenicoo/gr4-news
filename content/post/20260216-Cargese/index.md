> We are glad to announce that applications for the 2026 Edition of the Cargèse Summer School in Quantum Field Theory and Quantum Gravity are now open. This Advanced Summer School will take place from June 23 to July 3, 2026 at the Institut d’Etudes Scientifiques in Cargèse, a lovely village on the south-western coast of Corsica. The School is aimed at advanced doctoral students, postdoctoral fellows and junior researchers working in the field of high energy theory. Recent developments in string theory, quantum gravity and quantum field theory will be reviewed in pedagogical fashion, along with relations to quantum information, quantum many body systems,  cosmology, etc, see the school website for the list of lecturers.
> 
> The deadline for registration is March 20, 2026. PhD students should arrange for a letter of recommendation from their advisor to be sent to Cargese2026.references@cern.ch by the above deadline. The registration fee is 650 Euros, covering half board accommodation on a shared room basis. Funding will be available on specific request.
