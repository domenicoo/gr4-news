Dear Colleagues,

We are pleased to announce the conference Quantum Geometry and Physics, which will take place at Università del Piemonte Orientale, Vercelli, Italy, from June 15 to June 19, 2026.

Thursday, June 18, will feature a special Fest in honor of Leonardo Castellani.

The conference will bring together physicists and mathematicians to foster interactions on topics such as quantum groups, noncommutative geometry, supergeometry, and their applications in theoretical physics, including supersymmetry, supergravity, and quantum gravity.

For further details, please visit the conference webpage:
https://sites.google.com/view/quantumgeometryandphysics/home

If you plan to attend, we kindly ask you to register by April 27, 2026.

Please feel free to share this announcement with colleagues who may be interested.
