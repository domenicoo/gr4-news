> Dear Colleagues,
> 
> We are pleased to share the final announcement of the BiCoQ Conference: From
> Gravity to Particles, to be held at the University of Milano-Bicocca from
> June 15–19, 2026.
> 
> The registration is still open until the 1st of June. Moreover, we have
> reserved some slots on Thursday 18th for a poster session for which we will
> accept submissions until the 1st of June. We encourage especially PhD
> students and young postdocs to submit their contributions.
> 
> Here you can find the link to the conference
> webpage: https://indico.global/event/16054/
> and the full program:
> https://indico.global/event/16054/timetable/?view=standard
> 
> The conference, organized by the new Bicocca Center for Quantitative
> Cosmology (https://bicoq.fisica.unimib.it/) will bring together researchers
> working on gravity, cosmology, and fundamental physics, with a focus on the
> dark sector and new probes of physics beyond the Standard Model, through a
> combination of plenary talks and focused parallel sessions.
> 
> Keynote speakers:
> 
>  *  N. Fornengo (Universita' di Torino, Italy)
>  *  S. Vegetti (Max Planck Institute for Astrophysics, Germany)
>  *  S. Simon (Fermi National Accelerator Laboratory, USA)
>  *  A. Newman (Carnegie Science, USA)
>  *  U. Sperhake (University of Cambridge, UK)
>  *  N. Aggarwal (UC Davis, USA)
>  *  N. Luetzgendorf (ESA-ESTEC, Noordwijk, NL)
>  *  S. Fairhurst (University of Cardiff, UK
> 
> Best regards,
> The BiCoQ Organizing Committee
