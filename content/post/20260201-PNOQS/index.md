> Dear Colleagues,
> 
> Applications are now open for the international summer school "Physics
> of Nuclear Open Quantum Systems," which will take place at GANIL,
> Caen, France, from July 6 to July 24, 2026. We kindly ask faculty
> colleagues to circulate this announcement, and strongly encourage
> interested students to apply at:  
> https://indico.in2p3.fr/event/37944/
> 
> To receive full consideration, applications should be completed by
> March 15, 2026.
> 
> The school will focus on modern theoretical approaches to study weakly
> bound and unbound quantum many-body systems. While examples will be
> primarily drawn from the physics of atomic nuclei, the theoretical
> concepts introduced are broadly applicable to all open quantum
> systems. Selected experimental perspectives will be included.
