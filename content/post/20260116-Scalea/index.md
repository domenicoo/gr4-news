> Dear Colleagues,  
> we are pleased to announce the sixth edition of Estate Quantistica, which
> will take place in Scalea (Italy) from June 22 to June 26, 2026, at the
> Grand Hotel de Rose.  
> Estate Quantistica is an international scientific school and
> workshop devoted to recent developments in gravity, cosmology, and
> mathematical physics. The event is conceived as an advanced training
> opportunity, particularly aimed at graduate students, postdoctoral
> researchers, and early-career scientists, while remaining open to all
> researchers interested in these topics.  
> The scientific program consists of mini-courses delivered by leading
> experts, complemented by contributed talks and ample time for discussions.
> The invited lecturers for the 2026 edition include:
> -  Luca Amendola (Universität Heidelberg)
> -  Guido Carlet (Université Bourgogne Europe)
> -  Roberto Longo (University of Rome "Tor Vergata")
> -  Viatcheslav Mukhanov (Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität München)
> -  Dmitri Vassilevich (Universidade Federal do ABC)
> The school will be held in a particularly favourable setting, combining an
> intensive scientific program with an environment well suited for informal
> exchanges and collaboration among participants.  
> Further information on the event, including the scientific program,
> practical details, and registration procedures, can be found on the official
> webpage:  
> https://monge.u-bourgogne.fr/gdito/EQ2026/
> 
> We would be grateful if you could circulate this announcement among
> interested colleagues and students.
>
