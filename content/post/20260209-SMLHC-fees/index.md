> Dear all,

> As you may know, we will host the SM@LHC 2026 conference from April 7th to 10th at the Luigi Einaudi Campus. We would be very happy to have you join us, and we invite all local participants to register through the conference website:
> 
> https://agenda.infn.it/event/48435/registrations/5572/
>

> 
> After registering on the conference website, please register again at:
> 
> https://form.jotform.com/253003463246348
> 
> In the field "Institution/University*", enter "INFN — sezione di Torino". Then select "Invoice to INFN or other Italian public institution" and choose "bank transfer" as the payment method.
> 
> The bank transfer must be made to:  
> Beneficiary: Pragma Congressi  
> Bank : BPER  
> IBAN: IT48N0538711301000003606898  
> SWIFT:  BPMOIT22  
> Every bank transfer must mention ---SMLHC26--- as well as the ---name of the participant---
> 
> You should pay only the VAT‑exempt portion of the fee (the imponibile), which amounts to €204.92 for early registration and €245.90 for registrations after March 2nd.
> 
> If you need any additional information, please feel free to contact: eventi@pragmacongressi.it
