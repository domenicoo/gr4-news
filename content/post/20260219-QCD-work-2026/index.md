> Dear Colleague,
> 
> The 2026 edition of QCD@Work - International workshop on QCD, Theory and
> Experiment -
> (12th edition of the series) will be held in Trani (Italy) from 16th to 19th
> June 2026.
> 
> The topics are:
> 
> - Low Energy and nonperturbative QCD
> - Advances in Perturbative QCD
> - Heavy Quarks
> - QCD at extreme conditions of temperature and density
> - Holographic methods for strong interactions
> 
> The registrations are open at the workshop web page
> 
> https://agenda.infn.it/e/wqcd/2026
> 
> where information about venue, accommodation and organization is available.
> Call for abstracts is open.
> 
> We welcome your active participation in the meeting.
> We would also be grateful if you advertise this event to your interested
> colleagues.
> 
> Best regards,  
> The workshop organizers
