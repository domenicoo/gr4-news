> Dear Colleagues,
> 
> We are pleased to announce PAFT26 – Quantum Frontiers in Gravity, which will
> be held from March 29 to April 1, 2026, in Vietri sul Mare (SA), Italy.
> 
> The conference aims to bring together theoretical physicists and
> phenomenologists working at the interface of classical and quantum
> gravity, modified gravity, and experimental aspects of gravity in quantum
> systems. PAFT26 will provide a forum for constructive exchange of ideas and
> foster new collaborations across these complementary areas.
> 
> 
> The conference fee is 120 euros. Students may apply for a fee waiver during
> registration.
> 
> A Gala dinner is included in the social program at a cost of 70 euros,
> payable directly at the hotel.
> 
> 
> Important Dates
> 
>  *  Conference starts: Sunday, March 29, 2026, at 2:00 pm (after lunch)
>  *  Conference ends: Wednesday, April 1, 2026, at 1:00 pm (before lunch)
>  *  Deadline for talk submission: February 23, 2026
>  *  Notification of acceptance: February 28, 2026
>  *  Deadline for registration: March 13, 2026
> 
> A preliminary list of invited speakers includes:
> 
> G. Barnich (ULB), L.-Q. Chen (IQOQI), C. Curceanu (INFN), L. Diósi (Wigner
> RCP, Budapest),  
> J. Kowalski-Glikman (NCBJ & University of Wrocław), R. Loll (Radboud
> University & IMAPP),  
> N. Mavromatos (National Technical University of Athens & King’s College
> London),  
> A. Pachol (University of South-Eastern Norway), T. Sotiriou (University of
> Nottingham).
>
