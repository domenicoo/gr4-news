> Dear Colleague,
> 
> We are pleased to invite you to participate to the Neutrino Oscillation Workshop
> 2026 (NOW 2026), to be held at Basiliani Hotel & SPA (Otranto, Lecce, Italy)
> from August 31 (arrivals) to September 6 (departures). The Workshop will be
> organized in presence, with no remote talks. Apologies if you receive this
> message more than once.
> 
> You can find more information about NOW 2026 (and its previous editions) at:
> https://home.ba.infn.it/~now/now2026/
> Basic information on the scientific program, accommodation, fee, travel is
> already available on the webpages in the top menu. Further detailed info,
> including registration and fee+accommodation payment procedures, will be
> uploaded in due time.
> 
> The plenary program is almost completed and will be made public soon. Concerning
> the parallel program, please have a look at the Science "Topics" and "Program"
> webpages, where you can also find the conveners' names and email addresses for
> each session. If you (or one of your collaborators) intend to propose a talk in
> one of the parallel sessions, you are cordially invited to contact very soon
> (i.e., within the next few weeks) the appropriate couple of conveners by email,
> in order to propose the talk title and abstract. Please feel free to forward
> this information to distinguished colleagues that may be interested in joining
> NOW 2026 by applying to its parallel sessions.
> 
> For any communication or inquiry, please do not hesitate to contact us at
> now2026@ba.infn.it. Note that, due to constraints in accommodation, the overall
> attendance shall be limited to about 120 participants, with priority given to
> the selected session speakers.
> 
> We very much hope to see you at NOW 2026 in Otranto in the week from 31 August
> to 6 September.
> 
> Best Regards,
> 
> Antonio Marrone  
> Alessandro Mirizzi  
> Antonio Surdo  
> for the L.O.C. of NOW 2026
